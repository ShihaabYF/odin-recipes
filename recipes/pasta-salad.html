Awesome Pasta Salad

A delicious light Salad

This pasta salad recipe is easy to make.Made with simple ingredients, this salad is flexible, so you can make this when you are inundated by summer produce or adapt to what is in season. Plus, you are looking at under 20 minutes from start to finish. Not too bad, especially when you can keep it in the fridge for up to 24 hours!

ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fusilli (spiral) pasta
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 pound provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 pound salami, cubed
  • 1/4 pound sliced pepperoni, cut in half
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 (10 ounce) can black olives, drained
  • 1 (4 ounce) jar pimentos, drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle Italian salad dressing

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta.
  3. Cook it for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente.
  4. Drain, and rinse with cold water.
  5. Bring a large bowl.
  6. combine pasta with tomatoes, cheese, salami, pepperoni, green pepper, olives, and pimentos.
  7. Pour in salad dressing, and toss to coat.
